Getting started

Welcome to our platform! To get started, the first step is to log in to your account. If you're a returning user, simply enter your username and password on the login page. Login

For new users, you can easily create an account by clicking on the 'Sign Up' button and filling out the required information. Once logged in, you'll have access to a personalized experience, including features tailored to your preferences. If you encounter any issues, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team for assistance. Let's begin your journey with seamless access to our platform! signup

Choose format

Now that you've successfully logged in, it's time to customize your cricket experience. Step 2 involves selecting the format that suits your preferences. Whether you enjoy the fast-paced action of T20, the strategic play of One Day Internationals (ODIs), or the traditional and enduring Test matches, we've got you covered. Navigate to the 'Format Preferences' section on your dashboard, and simply choose your preferred cricket format. This ensures that you receive tailored updates, match schedules, and content that align with your cricketing interests. Embrace the excitement of your favorite format as you embark on an immersive cricketing journey with us!
